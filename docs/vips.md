# Logos Incubator Projects

Logos incubator projects are emerging initiatives that stem from [Logos Research's work](/research).
These projects are envisioned to eventually become separate IFT projects, benefiting from the support and resources of R&D Service Units within Logos Research.
The incubator projects operate within Logos Research, leveraging the expertise and collaboration available to them.

## Logos Execution Environment

[Logos Execution Environment](https://vac.dev/rlog/Nescience-state-separation-architecture) focuses on developing a state-separation architecture.
It enables privacy-preserving computing, which holds significant importance for [Logos](https://logos.co/) and other IFT projects.
